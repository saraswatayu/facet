# Product: Superhuman

Superhuman is a premium email client built for speed. It costs $30/month — in a world where Gmail, Outlook, and Apple Mail are free. The core promise is "the fastest email experience ever made": every interaction completes in under 100ms, the entire app is keyboard-driven, and AI features handle triage, writing, and summarization.

Users report saving 3-4 hours per week on email. The product has a famously selective onboarding process (1-on-1 concierge session) and strong word-of-mouth among startup founders and executives.

## Key Product Details

- $30/month per user (individual and team plans)
- Works with Gmail and Outlook accounts only
- AI features: auto-summarize threads, draft replies, instant triage, tone adjustment
- Keyboard-first: every action has a shortcut
- Split Inbox: automatic categorization into feeds
- Available on macOS, Windows, iOS, Android
- No free tier — $30/month or nothing

## Target Market

Professionals who spend 2+ hours/day in email and whose productivity directly impacts income — founders, salespeople, investors, consultants, recruiters, lawyers, PMs, executive assistants, freelancers.

## Options to Test

### Model A: $30/month flat (status quo)
One plan, one price, every feature. No free tier, no trials beyond onboarding. The price is the positioning signal.

### Model B: Free tier + $30/month Pro
Free tier with core speed and keyboard shortcuts. AI features gated behind $30/month Pro.

### Model C: $15/month Starter + $30/month Pro
Starter: core speed, Split Inbox, keyboard shortcuts. Pro: AI features, Read Statuses, Snippets, team features.
