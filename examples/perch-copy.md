## What's Being Tested

Which copy framing works best for the commission model? All variants below describe the same pricing (free signup, 15% commission on savings), but use different angles — aligned incentives, outcome-first, social proof, and concierge trust — to see which framing resonates most across segments.

## Copy Variants

### Variant B: "Commission, Aligned"
Free forever. We keep 15% when we save you money.
If we find nothing, you pay nothing. Start tracking — it's free.

CTA: "Start Tracking — It's Free"

### Variant C: "Commission, Ogilvy Flip"
Free until we save you money. You keep 85% of every drop.
We catch drops. You pocket the difference.

CTA: "Join the Waitlist"

### Variant D: "Outcome-First, Sierra-Style"
We only get paid when you save. 15% of what we find. You keep the rest.
If we find nothing, you pay nothing. Ever.
Pay for results, not promises.

CTA: "Start Tracking Free"

### Variant E: "Proof-First"
Most users save 3-5x what we charge. We take 15%. Only when it works.
The average Perch fee? $37/year. The average savings? $247.

CTA: "Start Tracking Free"

### Variant F: "Concierge Trust"
Add your flights. We handle the rest.
When prices drop, we rebook you and keep 15% of what we saved.
Like having a travel concierge who works on commission.

CTA: "Add Your First Flight"
