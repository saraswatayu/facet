# Product: Superhuman

Superhuman is a premium email client built for speed. $30/month in a world where Gmail is free. Every interaction under 100ms, keyboard-driven, AI triage and writing.

## Key Product Details

- $30/month per user, works with Gmail and Outlook
- AI features: summarize threads, draft replies, auto-triage
- Keyboard-first design, Split Inbox, Send Later, Read Statuses
- No free tier

## Target Market

Professionals who spend 2+ hours/day in email — founders, salespeople, investors, consultants, recruiters.

## Options to Test

### Model A: $30/month flat (status quo)
One plan, one price, every feature. The price is the positioning signal.

### Model B: Free tier + $30/month Pro
Free tier with core speed. AI features gated behind $30/month Pro.
