# Product: Perch

Perch is a flight price tracking and automatic rebooking service. After a user books a flight, they forward their confirmation email to Perch. Perch monitors the fare and if the price drops, automatically rebooks the user at the lower price.

The user does nothing after the initial setup — Perch handles the rebooking with the airline, and the user receives an email showing the savings.

## Key Product Details

- Works with all major US airlines (United, Delta, American, Southwest, Spirit, Frontier, JetBlue, Alaska)
- Handles the rebooking process automatically (no user action needed)
- Monitors both domestic and international flights
- Domestic flights: ~35% see a price drop before departure, average savings ~$55
- International flights: ~40% see a price drop, average savings ~$180
- Rebook success rate: 95% domestic, 90% international

## Target Market

Anyone in the US who books flights. Key segments to consider:
- Frequent flyers (business + personal)
- International travelers (immigrants visiting family, vacation travelers)
- Families booking multiple tickets
- Budget-conscious travelers
- Points/miles enthusiasts
- College students
- Retirees on fixed incomes
- Medical travelers
- Group travel organizers (weddings, events)
- People who don't currently track prices at all

## Options to Test

### Model A: $49/year flat fee

User pays $49/year upfront as an annual subscription. In exchange, Perch monitors all their flights and rebooks when prices drop. The user keeps 100% of all savings.

Money-back guarantee: if Perch finds no savings in 12 months, full refund.

Key dynamics:
- Upfront cost creates a conversion barrier
- Flat fee rewards high-volume flyers (capped cost)
- Simple ROI story: "I paid $49, saved $449, 9:1 return"
- Requires payment before any value is delivered

### Model B: Free signup, 15% commission

User signs up for free. No credit card required. Perch monitors flights and rebooks when prices drop. Perch keeps 15% of each savings event; the user gets 85%.

Key dynamics:
- Zero conversion barrier (free to try)
- Commission scales with value delivered
- User always nets positive (never pays more than they save)
- Higher-saving users pay more to Perch (uncapped)
- The word "free" is powerful in referral conversations

### Crossover Point

At $327 annual savings, the models are equivalent:
- Below $327: Model B is cheaper for the user (15% of $327 = $49)
- Above $327: Model A is cheaper for the user (flat $49 vs growing %)
